WHAT WE DO

At Greenlife mental health foundation, we provide psychosocial support and counseling services to the worst affected local communities living in the context affected by the ongoing insurgency and banditry within the North western part of Nigeria. We are also committed do providing Advocacy training, capacity building, outreach, research, educational seminars and lecture presentations. These are all in our mission to improve and promote the mental health and wellbeing of people in our local communities. At Greenlife mental health foundation, we have of team of mental health professionals to offer free psychotherapy to individuals and groups who may come down with:

  1. Anxiety and depression

  2. Mood swinging disorders

  3. Couple therapy

  4. Post-traumatic stress disorder management

  5. Addiction management

  6. Other mental health challenges

OUR MISSION

There is a growing commitment within the global mental health community in promoting the mental health and wellbeing of individuals living with mental health difficulties. GLMHF is aimed to nurture this commitment and hasten it through its objectives.
